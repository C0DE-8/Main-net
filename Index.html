How to Deposit Crypto

Follow these steps to safely deposit crypto into your wallet:

  1. Access Your Wallet: Open your crypto wallet and navigate to the "Deposit" section.
  2. Obtain Your Address: Copy your wallet's deposit address or scan the provided QR code. Always verify the address before proceeding.
  3. Initiate the Transfer: Send the desired amount from your exchange or another wallet to your deposit address.
  4. Confirm the Transaction: Wait for the required network confirmations for your funds to appear in your account.
  5. Monitor Your Balance: Once confirmed, your deposited funds will reflect in your wallet.

Please double-check your wallet address and transaction details. Contact support immediately if you encounter any issues.
